\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

Across both probes and both chains the calibration cohorts recover the expected
order, and the immrep25 positives fall at the bottom, on top of the
$P_{\text{gen}}$-matched random floor (Fig.~\ref{fig:ladder}). Their only
measurable homology is contributed by public TCRs and vanishes when those are
removed; their chain pairing shows no coupling beyond marginals. We therefore find
\emph{no evidence of epitope-specific signal---in homology or chain pairing---among
the immrep25 positives beyond noise and publicity}.
